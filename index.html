Camp Along East Lake Toho’s Scenic Waterfront

East Toho RV Resort and Marina is nestled on the northern shore of East Lake Tohopekaliga, affectionately referred to as East Lake Toho. The community is replete with old-Florida Oaks, pristine waters, spacious RV sites, and vacation rental cottages. The Resort is currently undergoing a multimillion dollar enhancement across each facet of the community, but we are open and welcome you to take full advantage of our onsite features, including:

Lakeside RV sites and views (Pavered Supersites now available!)

Rental cottages (upgraded units available by February 2022)

Swimming pool (opening March 2022)

On-site marina and boat launch

Gorgeous grounds with mature oak trees Fishing

Convenience store

Laundry facilities

New Supersite Winter Special – $1200/month

Includes access to our refurbished supersite bathhouse!
